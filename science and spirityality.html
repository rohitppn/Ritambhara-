Research Areas of Ritambhara

Mantras and Waves

Our research explores the intriguing connection between ancient mantras and consciousness. Recent studies have delved into the vibrational patterns of mantras and their impact on neural oscillations. By analyzing brainwave frequencies during mantra recitation, we have gained insights into the therapeutic potential of sound waves, paving the way for novel applications in mental health and well-being.

Ritambhara Research Institute fosters a dynamic dialogue between science and spirituality. Neuroscience and Consciousness

Ritambhara's expertise lies in the intersection of neuroscience and consciousness research. Our interdisciplinary approach has resulted in significant strides, mapping neural pathways associated with consciousness. Recent findings highlight the role of specific brain regions in shaping subjective experiences, offering valuable insights into the neural basis of awareness and self-perception.
